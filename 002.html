Leading People: Conflict Management

Think about the last 2­3 meetings you have attended. In these meetings, did you disagree with anything said in those meetings? What causes you to speak up and what causes you to hold back at times? Do you have the feeling that other participants were holding back from voicing their opinions?

At the next meeting you attend, strive to voice your opinion on an issue that matters to you. If you are someone who voices your opinion freely, check the room to notice if there is someone who might be holding back their opinion.
